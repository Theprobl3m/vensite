History of Jos, Plateau state

Plateau state is one of the 36 states in Nigeria,Located in the middle-belt of the country. The capital of the state is Jos and it is known as the home of peace and tourism. It is a beautiful state with natural endowments and attractions to tourists mostly within Nigeria. Known for its amazing climate,plateau state is a favourite location for people from different parts of the country to explore various business opportunities.
